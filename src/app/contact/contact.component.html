Kansas City Women in Technology

Kansas City Women in Technology(KCWiT) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization aimed at growing the number of women in technology careers in the Kansas City area. They provide networking events and volunteer opportunities for members, and run programs targeted towards youth to encourage girls to pursue technology.
